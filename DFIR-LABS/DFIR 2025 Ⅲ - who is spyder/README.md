you can either directly answer it or you can solve the challenge by running main.py in Solution folder and answering it.

```

Q2) What is the host used by the encrypted channel?
Format: 192.168.128.155

Q3) What are the encryption algorithm, encryption mode, key, and iv used in the encrypted channel?
Format: des_cfb_d7366d6d6d9676e6_782d6d732d6d6574
```
